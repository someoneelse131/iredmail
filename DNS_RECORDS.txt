DNS RECORDS FOR kirby.rocks MAIL SERVER
========================================

Replace YOUR_SERVER_IP with your actual server's public IP address.

REQUIRED RECORDS
----------------

1. A Record (mail server hostname)
   Type: A
   Name: mail
   Value: YOUR_SERVER_IP
   TTL: 3600

2. MX Record (mail exchanger)
   Type: MX
   Name: @ (or kirby.rocks)
   Value: mail.kirby.rocks
   Priority: 10
   TTL: 3600

3. SPF Record (sender policy framework)
   Type: TXT
   Name: @ (or kirby.rocks)
   Value: v=spf1 mx ~all
   TTL: 3600

4. DKIM Record (will be generated after first startup)
   Type: TXT
   Name: dkim._domainkey
   Value: (check /var/lib/dkim/kirby.rocks.pem after container starts)
   TTL: 3600

5. DMARC Record
   Type: TXT
   Name: _dmarc
   Value: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:postmaster@kirby.rocks
   TTL: 3600

OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED
------------------------

6. Reverse DNS (PTR Record)
   Configure with your hosting provider to point YOUR_SERVER_IP to mail.kirby.rocks

7. Autodiscover (for email clients)
   Type: CNAME
   Name: autodiscover
   Value: mail.kirby.rocks
   TTL: 3600

   Type: CNAME
   Name: autoconfig
   Value: mail.kirby.rocks
   TTL: 3600

GETTING DKIM KEY
----------------
After starting the container, get your DKIM public key:

  docker exec iredmail cat /var/lib/dkim/kirby.rocks.pem

Then create the DNS TXT record with the key value.

TESTING YOUR SETUP
------------------
After DNS propagation (can take up to 48 hours), test with:
- https://mxtoolbox.com/
- https://www.mail-tester.com/
